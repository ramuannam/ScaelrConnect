Why this DSA SHEET?

After lot of questioning to the scaler students and from their feedback, we found that lot of people are struggling to RECALL/REVISE the concepts they have learned and it is only possible through active practice and time lapsed recall so we the November-2022 batch decided to group up the problems that are in the scaler curriculum and bit of extra questions from the leetcode and grouped based on the difficulty.
